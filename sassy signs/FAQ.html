Frequently Asked Questions

What are your rates for more than one sign?

Our signs are $65 for the first sign and $10 each additional sign. This price included personalization and delivery within a 15 mile radius of I85/385 in Greenville, SC. If it is outside that delivery area, there is an additional charge of $1 per mile for setup and pickup. For our baby announcement signs, they are $85 and that includes a medallion for them to keep personalized with the birth information.

How are signs made?

We hand make each sign from scratch! We purchase 4x8 sheets of plywood. We find what graphic we want to make, project it on the wood, trace it out and cut it with a jigsaw. We then sand and paint the sign and add our personal, decorative touches to each one.

I would like to request a custom sign. How do I do that?

Call, message or email us and we will customize a sign for your special celebration! Please fill out the contact form below in order to inquiry about any of our signs on our catalog!

What time will you get to my house to set up and take down the signs?

These signs stay up for approximately 48 hours. We come the night before to set them up and leave a door tag after our service is complete! Baby signs stay up for about 5 days.
