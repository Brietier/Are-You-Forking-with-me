OUR ORIGIN STORY

The Joyful Heroes of Greenville, SC started their heroic journey during 2020, where the dastardly villian, COVID-19, had the world under attack. Our signage heroes, Anne and Harper, are the best of friends and made it their duty and mission to bring joy and happiness to the people of Greenville and all surrounding parts by finding ways to connect with others without suspecting the villanious COVID-19.

Harper is the wife of David and they have 2 daughters, one at Clemson and one at USC. Harper is a caregiver and has her own decorating business. Anna is the wife of Kemper and has 1 son who is at USC and a daughter at Eastside High School. She is a substitute preschool teacher and stay at home mom.

Their Heroic Declaration: Our signs are different from all other companies in our area! We hand make and design each one ourselves. We can do any celebration you would like. We love making people smile and spreading joy!
